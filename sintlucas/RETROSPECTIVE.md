# RETROSPECTIVES

## Introduction
Have a discussion with all team members about the following topics:
1. On a scale of 1–10, how do you feel this sprint went? Why?
2. If you could go back in time and do this sprint again, what would you change and what wouldn't you change?
3. What about this sprint was the biggest source of stress or difficulty for you personally?
4. How did you cope with things that went wrong?
5. What was the effect of earlier retrospectives?
6. Are there aspects of our team culture that can be improved?
